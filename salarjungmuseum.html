Salar Jung Museum

Explore the finest art collection in Hyderabad

The Salar Jung Museum is one of the largest museums in India, housing an extensive collection of art, antiques, and sculptures. Located near the Musi River, it showcases artifacts from all over the world, including Persian carpets, sculptures, and rare manuscripts.

It is famous for its incredible collection of European and Asian art, including works by Leonardo da Vinci and sculptures from the Mughal period.
