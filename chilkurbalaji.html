Chilkur Balaji Temple

A unique temple with a distinct pilgrimage

Chilkur Balaji Temple, located near the Osman Sagar Lake, is famous for its pilgrimage, where devotees take rounds around the temple as a form of prayer. It is one of the oldest temples in Hyderabad, dedicated to Lord Venkateshwara.

The temple is popular for its simple and unique rituals, and visitors often flock here to seek blessings for their wishes.
