Golconda Fort

A majestic fortress with a rich history

Golconda Fort is a historic citadel and fort located just outside the city. It was originally built in the 12th century and expanded by the Qutb Shahi dynasty in the 16th century. The fort is famous for its acoustics and the sound of claps that echo within its walls.

Visitors can explore its impressive architecture, including the grand gateways, ancient temples, and the stunning view from the top of the fort.
