Charminar

One of the most iconic landmarks in Hyderabad

The Charminar is a historical mosque and a signature monument of Hyderabad. Located in the heart of the old city, it is known for its grand arches and four towering minarets. Built in 1591 by Sultan Muhammad Quli Qutb Shah, it remains a symbol of the city.

Visitors can explore the vibrant market surrounding Charminar and shop for traditional jewelry, fabric, and spices.
