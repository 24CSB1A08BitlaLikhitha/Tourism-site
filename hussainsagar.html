Hussain Sagar Lake

A serene and picturesque lake in the heart of Hyderabad

Hussain Sagar is an artificial lake that connects the twin cities of Hyderabad and Secunderabad. Built in 1562, the lake is famous for the large statue of Buddha located on an island in the middle.

Visitors can take boat rides on the lake, visit the Buddha statue, or enjoy a peaceful walk along the picturesque promenade surrounding the water.
