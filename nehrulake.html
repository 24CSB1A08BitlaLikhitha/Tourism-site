Nehru Zoological Park

A sanctuary for wildlife lovers

Nehru Zoological Park, established in 1959, is one of the largest zoos in India. Located near the Mir Alam Tank, it houses a wide variety of animals including tigers, lions, elephants, and more. The zoo is also home to a butterfly park and a natural history museum.

Visitors can enjoy safari tours, walk through the animal enclosures, and learn about conservation efforts in the park.
